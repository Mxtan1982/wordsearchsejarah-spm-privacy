# Privacy Policy for Sejarah Word Search

Last updated: 15 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how **Sejarah Word Search** ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 No Personal Account Data
We do **not** require you to create an account and we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, IC, or school details.

### 1.2 Device & Usage Data (via Google / AdMob)
Our app uses **Google AdMob** to show ads. AdMob may automatically collect:
- Device information (such as device model, OS version, language, IP address)
- Advertising ID (for example, Google Advertising ID)
- General usage data (such as how often the app is used)

This data is collected and processed by Google according to their own privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

We, as the developer, do **not** receive data that can directly identify you personally.

## 2. How We Use Information

The information collected via AdMob is used mainly to:
- Display and improve ads (personalized or non-personalized, depending on user/Google settings)
- Improve app stability and performance (through aggregated analytics)

We do **not** sell your personal data to any third party.

## 3. Children’s Privacy

Our app is designed mainly for **students and teachers** as an educational tool.

If you are under 18, you should use this app under the guidance of a teacher, parent, or guardian.

We do **not** knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob** (mobile advertising)
- **Google Play Services** (basic Android services)

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

AdMob Policy:
https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

## 5. Data Security

We do not store any sensitive personal data on our own servers.  
Data handled by Google/AdMob is protected by their own security measures.

## 6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal account information, we do not keep user profiles or login data.

Advertising and analytics data is stored by Google/AdMob according to their own retention policies.

## 7. Your Choices

- You can **turn off personalized ads** in your device’s Google settings (Ads / Advertising ID).
- You can uninstall the app at any time if you do not agree with this Privacy Policy.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
